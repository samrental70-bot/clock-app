QuickBooks MCP Privacy Policy

Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD | Effective date: June 30, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD handles information processed by its QuickBooks MCP integration. The integration is for internal company accounting automation only. It is not a public consumer application and does not provide public user signup.

1. Information Processed

When authorized, the integration may read accounting information from QuickBooks Online, including customer records, invoices, unpaid invoices, expenses, transactions, profit and loss reports, balance sheet reports, company profile information, and related accounting metadata.

2. Purpose of Processing

Information is processed only for Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD internal business purposes, including accounting review, reporting, reconciliation, cash-flow visibility, invoice review, and operational automation.

3. No Public Signup

The integration is not available for public registration or general customer use. Access is limited to authorized Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD personnel or approved service providers.

4. No Sale of Customer Data

Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD does not sell customer data collected, accessed, or processed by this QuickBooks MCP integration.

5. Read-Only First Operation

The integration begins in read-only mode. No QuickBooks data should be created, edited, deleted, sent, voided, or otherwise modified through the integration unless Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD ownership has approved the specific write action capability.

6. Credentials and Tokens

QuickBooks OAuth credentials and tokens are treated as confidential operational secrets. They must not be placed in frontend code, public pages, public repositories, browser bundles, or logs intended for public access.

7. Sharing and Service Providers

Accounting data may be processed by authorized systems and service providers needed to run the integration, such as Intuit QuickBooks Online and internal automation infrastructure. Data is not shared for unrelated advertising or resale purposes.

8. Retention

Accounting records remain governed by Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD business, tax, and legal retention requirements. Local tokens or operational logs should be retained only as long as needed for secure operation, troubleshooting, audit, or compliance.

9. Security

Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD uses reasonable administrative and technical safeguards for this internal integration, including restricted access, secret handling practices, and separation of backend credentials from frontend code.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy or the QuickBooks MCP integration should be directed to Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD ownership or administration.
