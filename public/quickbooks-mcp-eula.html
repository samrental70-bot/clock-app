QuickBooks MCP End User License Agreement

Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD | Effective date: June 30, 2026

This End User License Agreement governs use of the Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD QuickBooks MCP integration. The integration is an internal company accounting automation tool that connects authorized company systems to QuickBooks Online through the Intuit QuickBooks Online API.

1. Internal Business Use Only

The QuickBooks MCP integration is provided only for Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD internal accounting, reporting, reconciliation, and operational automation. It is not a public consumer application and does not offer public user signup.

2. Authorized Users

Access is limited to authorized Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD owners, administrators, employees, or approved contractors who need accounting access for legitimate company purposes. Unauthorized access or use is prohibited.

3. Read-Only First Design

The integration is designed to begin in read-only mode. It may read approved QuickBooks Online accounting data such as customers, invoices, expenses, transactions, profit and loss reports, and balance sheet reports.

4. No Write Actions Without Owner Approval

The integration must not create, edit, delete, void, send, approve, post, or otherwise modify QuickBooks Online data unless Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD ownership has reviewed and approved the specific write capability. Any future write actions must include a clear permission layer before they are enabled.

5. Data Handling

The integration is intended only to process company accounting information needed for internal business operations. Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD does not sell customer data through this integration.

6. Credentials and Security

QuickBooks credentials, OAuth tokens, client secrets, and refresh tokens must be kept out of frontend code and public repositories. Access tokens and refresh tokens should be stored securely and used only for authorized accounting automation.

7. Third-Party Services

This integration connects to QuickBooks Online and may depend on Intuit services, APIs, and authorization flows. Use of QuickBooks Online remains subject to Intuit's applicable terms and policies.

8. No Warranty

The integration is provided for internal operational use. To the maximum extent permitted by law, it is provided without warranties of uninterrupted availability, error-free operation, or suitability for any particular accounting outcome.

9. Contact

Questions about this agreement or the QuickBooks MCP integration should be directed to Ottawa Renovation Pro LTD ownership or administration.
