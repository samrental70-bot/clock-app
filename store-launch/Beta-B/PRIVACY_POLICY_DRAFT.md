## What Data We Collect
Depending on user role, company settings, and enabled features, the app may collect:
- Name.
- Email address.
- Role and company membership.
- Clock-in and clock-out times.
- Project, job site, task, and cost centre information.
- GPS/location data at clock-in/out and live location if enabled.
- Job-site photos.
- Receipt images and receipt metadata.
- Schedule/task responses such as accepted or declined assignments.
- Device, browser, and technical data needed to operate the app.

## Why We Collect Data
We collect this information to:
- Track employee time.
- Support payroll and labour reporting.
- Document project and job-site activity.
- Track receipts and expenses by project.
- Provide supervisor and owner visibility.
- Support safety, accountability, and operational coordination.
- Maintain app reliability and security.

## Who Can See Data
- Employees can see their own allowed records.
- Supervisors and owners can see company/team records according to role permissions.
- Access is controlled by company membership and RBAC rules.
