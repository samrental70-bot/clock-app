# Store Asset Checklist

## Common Assets Needed
- App icon source SVG/PNG.
- Adaptive Android icon foreground/background.
- iOS app icon 1024x1024.
- Splash screen icon/background.
- Screenshots.
- Google Play feature graphic.
- Privacy policy hosted URL.
- Support URL.
- Optional marketing website URL.
- Demo login credentials.
- App description.
- Release notes.
- Contact email.
- Company legal name.
- Bundle/package ID.
- App version/build number.

## Apple-Specific
- Apple Developer Program account.
- Bundle ID.
- App Store Connect app record.
- iOS build archive or wrapper build.
- Signing certificates/profiles managed by Xcode if applicable.
- App privacy answers.
- Review notes.
- Screenshots/app previews.

## Google-Specific
- Google Play Developer account.
- Package name.
- Android App Bundle `.aab`.
- Keystore/upload key stored securely outside repo.
- Play App Signing decision.
- Data Safety form.
- Content rating questionnaire.
- Privacy policy URL.
- Screenshots.
- Feature graphic.
- Internal testing track.
