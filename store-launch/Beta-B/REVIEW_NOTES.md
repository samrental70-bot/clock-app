# Store Review Notes Draft

## App Purpose
OPERA.AI is a field operations and time tracking app for contractor businesses. It helps employees clock in and out, review schedules, submit job-site photos and receipts, and review their own timesheets. Managers and owners can review team activity according to role permissions.

## Login Requirement
Login is required to access the app. Store reviewers need a demo account.

## Reviewer Test Flow
1. Log in.
2. Open Clock and review clock-in/out.
3. Open Schedule and review assigned/upcoming tasks.
4. Open Timesheets and review labour records.
5. Open Photos and review job-site media.
6. Open Receipts and review expense capture.
7. Open Team/Menu and review role-appropriate tools.

## Permission Explanations
- Location: supports job-site verification during clock-in/out and live team visibility when enabled.
- Camera/photos: supports job-site photo capture and receipt uploads.
- Notifications: supports schedule/task reminders when enabled.

## Audience
This app is intended for employees and managers of a business. It is not a general public social app.
