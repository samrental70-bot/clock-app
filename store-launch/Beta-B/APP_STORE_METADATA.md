## Description Draft
OPERA.AI is a field operations and time tracking app for contractors and service teams. Teams can clock in and out from the job site, review schedules, track labour hours and costs, capture job-site photos, upload receipts, and keep managers aligned with live crew status.

Beta Release B introduces the Royal Navy Luxury interface, improved mobile navigation, a cleaner clock-in experience, polished timesheets, and professional field documentation screens for photos and receipts.

Managers and supervisors can review company activity based on their role. Employees see the tools they need for clocking in, assigned schedules, and their own timesheets.

## App Review Notes
This is a field operations and time tracking app for employees, supervisors, and owners of a business. The app is not a public social network. A demo account should be provided so reviewers can access the operational UI after login.

Suggested review flow:
1. Log in with the provided demo account.
2. Open Clock and review clock-in / clock-out flow.
3. Open Schedule and review upcoming tasks.
4. Open Timesheets and review labour records.
5. Open Photos and Receipts to review job-site documentation.
6. Open More/Menu to review available tools.
